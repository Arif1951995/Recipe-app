Cooking Direction

  1. For Marination:
  2. Marinate the chicken breasts with oyster sauce, salt, black pepper and mustard.
  3. Let it marinate for 1/2 hour.
  4. In another bowl put batter ingredients except bread crumbs, mix thoroughly and make batter.
  5. Dip the chicken breasts in the prepared batter then coat by breadcrumbs and then deep fry until cooked.:
  6. Take bun cut between and spread mayonnaise on the buns.:
  7. Put a leaf of lettuce and fried chicken breast in bun and tomato close bun and serve.
